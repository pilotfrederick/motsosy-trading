Electrical Repairs

Facing electrical problems? At Motsosy Trading, we provide fast, reliable, and long-lasting electrical repair services. Our team of experienced technicians is equipped to handle everything from minor fixes to complex troubleshooting.

We Repair:

  • Faulty wiring and electrical outlets
  • Tripped breakers and overloaded circuits
  • Malfunctioning lighting systems and fixtures
  • Electrical appliances and equipment

Our goal is to minimize downtime and restore power quickly while ensuring all repairs comply with safety standards.
